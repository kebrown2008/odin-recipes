Red Coconut Curry with Shrimp

This is a delicous Coconut Red Curry dish with shrimp added to it for a delicious taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 medium white onion sliced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into thin strips

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, and jalepeno.
  3. Cook until softened, 4-5 minutes.
