Air Fryer Chicken Parmesan

This is a delicous and simple way to make Chicken Parmesan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 8oz package of spaghetti
  • 1/2 tbs salt
  • 2 pre-cooked breaded chicken fillets

Steps

  1. Pre-heat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 C) for 5 minutes.
  2. Boil and salt a large pot of water.
  3. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
