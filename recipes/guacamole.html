Guacamole

This is a delicous and simple Guacamole recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 5 mashed avacodos
  • 2 tbs fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced green onion

Steps:

  1. Stir avacodos and lemon juice in a serving bowl.
  2. Add green onion and cilantro.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
